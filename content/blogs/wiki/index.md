## Source Coverage

The wiki draws primarily from posts across 2017–2026. Older posts (2017–2022) tend toward pastoral theology and practical ethics. Posts from 2025–2026 show increasing focus on [[knowing-god]] as the explicit frame for everything else, and a growing engagement with AI and technology (see [[wisdom]]).

The "life gauges" framework (2019) and the "sillage" metaphor (2017, revisited 2025) are the blog's most developed original frameworks.
