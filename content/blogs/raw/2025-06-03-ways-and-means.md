# Ways and Means

I planted, Apollos watered, but God gave the increase.  
⏤ 1 Cor. 3:6

When my focus is on results, I will clamor for the biggest levers of power. But when I prioritize process, I can be free to leverage every small moment. Give the gift of myself to the person in my orbit. Whisper a prayer. God doesn't call me to *change* the world, but he does call me to *love* the world. It's a test of trust to embrace his ways and means while surrendering results to him.
