# Reality Has a Point

We all fell to the ground, and I heard a voice saying to me in Aramaic, ‘Saul, Saul, why do you persecute me? It is hard for you to kick against the goads.’ ⏤ Acts 26:14

We fight it. We ignore it. We escape it. We reimagine it. But reality wins in the end. Get that noise checked out. Fix the leak. Listen to your conscience. It's painful to kick against the pointy prod of truth.
