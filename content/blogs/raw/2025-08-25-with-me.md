# With Me

“Whoever is not with me is against me, and whoever does not gather with me scatters." — Luke 11:23

Religion has its own swift current. Routinely, I need to paddle into a quiet eddy and reflect: How is my relationship *with* the Christ of Christianity? Are my aims consistent with his? Am I gathering with him, to him?
