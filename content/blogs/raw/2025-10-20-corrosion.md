# Corrosion

But he said to me, “My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness.” — 2 Corinthians 12:9a

If I am being corroded by the environment, I have three options:

1. Apply paint. Guard myself against the elements and live a shielded life.
2. Go inside. Withdraw and live a solitary life.
3. Be made of better stuff. Show up ready and be who I've been made to be, completed by God's grace.
