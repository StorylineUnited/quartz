# Real Change

Do not conform to the pattern of this world, but be transformed by the renewing of your mind. Then you will be able to test and approve what God’s will is—his good, pleasing and perfect will. ⏤ Romans 12:2

Do not conform. Be transformed. Renew your mind. Formation is the *process* of moving from conformity in one pattern to conformity in another. Transformation is the deep-seated *result* of that process. Heart, soul, mind, and strength catch up to a change in identity.
