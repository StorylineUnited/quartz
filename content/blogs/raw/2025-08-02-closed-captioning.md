# Closed Captioning

Uh, what did he say?

My life's broadcast can be hard to interpret, even for me. Attempts to patiently leaven culture can seem like mumbling. I still carry a thick accent from the dominion I was rescued from.

It's partly why I write. A transcript helps bring clarity.
