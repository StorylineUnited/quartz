# Taken By Surprise

Today I had the amazing privilege of baptizing one of my nephews. He's always been a young man I've been proud of. But many wonderful people don't claim to follow Jesus. Part of his story is that he didn't grow up in church and was never very interested in church. So I was surprised to hear him share a testimony that was as genuine and compelling as I've ever heard.
