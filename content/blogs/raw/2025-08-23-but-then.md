# But Then

Let the one who is wise heed these things  
 and ponder the loving deeds of the Lord. ⏤ Psalm 107:43

I love this psalm. How many different ways can we find ourselves in trouble? Desperate. And God meets us in all of them. His love can be found. Wise people look for it and dwell on it. Then, maybe they write psalms to tell others.
