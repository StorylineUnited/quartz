# Blessed

Blessed is the man who trusts in the Lord and whose trust is the Lord. — Jer. 17:7

Faith is trust that's able to take a step. When we take that step, we courageously exercise our trust, drawing on God's grace. Our faith might be small as a tiny seed, but it delights God because we are moving with him in blessing. And he makes it all possible.
