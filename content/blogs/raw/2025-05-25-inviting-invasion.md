# Inviting Invasion

Your kingdom come. [God is altogether other](__GHOST_URL__/dailies/altogether-other/). His kingdom is a rule of love. This kingdom comes as more and more people surrender to his rule in their work, play, relationships — all of life. That seems *other*. That seems like one culture invading another. With God-like, Jesus-like love, of course!
