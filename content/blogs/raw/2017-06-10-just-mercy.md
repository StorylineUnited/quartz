# Just mercy

The verdict is about to be delivered. Will the judge serve up justice? Or will she extend mercy?

Are justice and mercy opposites? Or can they be paired? In Matthew, Joseph shows us justice and mercy can share the same space. It is possible to regard truth *and* regard a person's God-given dignity. We can shine light into shadowy places without parading the Enemy's captives down Social Media Street.
