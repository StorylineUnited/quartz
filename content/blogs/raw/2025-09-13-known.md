# Known

I am the good shepherd; I know my sheep and my sheep know me—just as the Father knows me and I know the Father—and I lay down my life for the sheep. — John 10:14-15

"I know my sheep and my sheep know me." We've been told that Christianity isn't a religion, but a relationship. Do we really believe it? Jesus' language here is so personal and relational. This is what [eternal life](__GHOST_URL__/what-is-eternal-life/) looks like.
