# Seek, Dwell, Gaze, Seek

One thing I ask from the Lord, this only do I seek: that I may dwell in the house of the Lord all the days of my life, to gaze on the beauty of the Lord and to seek him in his temple. — Psalm 27:4

This is the invitation to people who like action words. It's pursuit, and desire, and wonder that fuels faithful participation with God, even when participation looks like repentance.
