# Time Lapse Formation

All of us have learned. We all have come to new conclusions. When we change our minds, people need us to show our work. Those who see an abrupt change could think we have just 'switched sides'. Without a view into the process, they will write a story to make sense of things. "Did it for money." "Did it to fit in." A lot happened between the two snapshots. Share the process. It might be instructive. It might even be persuasive.
