# The yeast of reality

Does our view of reality come from:

A. What we experience on Sunday? Are people and the surrounding culture becoming more like Christ and his kingdom?

B. What we experience Monday through Saturday? Are the people of our churches becoming more like the surrounding culture?

Studies say the answer is more B than A.
