# The First Story

Long before ‘God With Us’ (Immanuel), God dwelt with his people. But differently. If our pursuit is intimacy with God, then the Old Testament has great value. It’s a record—the first story—of God dwelling with his people.

In Christ, Gentiles have been counted with his Chosen. The early Scriptures reveal our Father and point to a Deliverer who came to bring us home.
