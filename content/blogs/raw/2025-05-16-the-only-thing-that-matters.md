# The Only Thing That Matters

The only thing that counts is faith expressing itself through love. — Gal. 5:6b

Pastor Mike Ashcraft often says, "Left and Right are two sides of the same coin in the wrong system." Here, Paul says faith and love are two sides of the same coin in the *right* system.
