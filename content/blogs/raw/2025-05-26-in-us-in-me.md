# In Us. In Me.

For this is the will of God, your sanctification. — 1 Thess. 4:3a

Your will be done. I remember Henry Blackaby teaching in *Experiencing God* that "What is God's will for my life?" is the wrong question. The right question is "What is God's will?" God's will is for his people to be [holy as he is holy](__GHOST_URL__/dailies/altogether-other/).

Paul's letters were written to churches, God's called-out people, Christ's body. If God's will is done, it will be seen in us, the Church.
