# About &amp; More

Eternal life is [knowing God](__GHOST_URL__/dailies/what-is-eternal-life/). What is it to know God? Is it enough to know *about* him? Think of people you know about: sports figures, performers, business leaders, politicians, people in gossip columns or the rumor mill. Now bring to mind people you know about AND know. How would you describe the difference? What is the 'more' in knowing?
